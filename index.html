Cryptocurrency Flow Investigation

Cryptocurrencies run on decentralized blockchain technologies, making them difficult to investigate in the traditional way. The goal of this solution is to offer a systematic way to investigate the movement and flow of cryptocurrencies, providing transparency, and meeting regulatory requirements. The framework focuses on monitoring and analyzing cryptocurrency transactions, identifying and preventing money laundering and fraudulent activities, enhancing cooperation between regulators, law enforcement, and cryptocurrency platforms, and improving security measures to protect against cyber threats.
